Pierre & Tim

This project is a small campaign for the cookie merchants Pierre et Tim. I collaborated with Emma Bibal. Together, we created a typography inspired by cookie dough, flyers, banners, and a promotional video highlighting the store. The tone is light and playful. The video was created using After Effects and Photoshop.
