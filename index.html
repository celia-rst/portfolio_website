This opening sequence was conceived as an introduction for contemporary viewers to a vintage film.

Hand-animated, the elements gradually appear on screen, echoing the film's synopsis of a village that appears and disappears every hundred years.

The technique used is hand-painted animation, bringing poetry and dreamlike qualities to the sequence.

Envol

"Envol" is my final animation cinema project. It was created using 2D animation on TV Paint, with backgrounds designed in vector format using Illustrator. The aim of this film was to poetically evoke the concept of evolution through the metaphor of bird migration. My artistic objective for this project was to enhance the visuals with hand-made textures, adding them to a primarily digital base. Therefore, I worked on my images through compositing to achieve a unique visual outcome.
